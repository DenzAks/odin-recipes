Zucchini Pizza Bites

Ideal for a healthy snack

This zucchini pizza bites recipe tops slices of fresh zucchini with zesty pizza sauce, gooey cheese and pepperoni for an easy bite.

  • 2 zucchini, slices into 1/4 inch thick rounds
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
  • 20 slices pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Italian seasoning, dried parsley, oregano for sprinkling

How to

  1. Preheat the oven 375 degrees F(190 degrees C). Line a baking a sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Spread zucchini slices 1 layer on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until zucchini is tender, about 15 minutes.
  4. Spoon pizza sauce over each zucchini slice and top with pepperoni and mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle herbs over each.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes more.
