Sausage Balls Recipe

Can be made in mere minutes to form bite-sized snacks that are full of flavor

Sausage balls are a holiday staple as an appetizer and also grace many a table for holiday breakfast as well!

  • Sausage
  • Baking mix
  • Cheese
  1. Mix the sausage and the baking mix together.
  2. Stir in the cheese.
  3. Roll the mixture into the balls. Transfer to a baking sheet.
  4. Bake until the balls are the golden brown and the sausage is cooked through.
